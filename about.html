About Tyler

Tyler is a teenage composer. He started music composition in 2020, and so far, he's composed over 20 songs.
He started studying piano when he was 2 years old. He studies at the Music Institute of North Texas, with Dr. Imre Pátkai.
He recently was accepted into the NowBeat Project.
In addition to composing music, Tyler likes video games, Microsoft Flight Simulator, his dog (Biscuit), mountain biking, NTPA Studios, and playing piano.
